## Success metrics (V1)

> GUIDANCE: 3–5 leading indicators that tell you if this is working in the first 90 days post-launch. Be quantitative. "User loves it" is not a metric.

| Metric | Target | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Onboarding completion rate | ≥ 60% | If <60%, the funnel is broken before the value moment |
| Paywall conversion (trial start) | ≥ 8% | Cal AI hits ~10–15%; <5% means paywall or onboarding fails |
| Trial-to-paid conversion | ≥ 50% | Industry benchmark for hard-paywall consumer apps |
| Day 7 retention (paid) | ≥ 40% | Predicts LTV |
| App Store rating | ≥ 4.5 stars | <4.5 kills ASO and word-of-mouth |
